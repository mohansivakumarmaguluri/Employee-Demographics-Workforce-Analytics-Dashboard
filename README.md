#  Employee Demographics Workforce Analytics Dashboard

An interactive **HR Analytics Dashboard** built using **Power BI** to analyze workforce demographics, employee distribution, salaries, leave balance, hiring trends, and job roles. The dashboard provides HR professionals and business managers with key insights to support data-driven workforce planning and decision-making.

---

##  Project Overview

The HR Analytics Dashboard offers a comprehensive view of employee data through interactive visualizations and KPIs. It enables users to monitor workforce composition, analyze employee demographics, evaluate salary distribution, and track hiring trends over time.

The dashboard includes:
- Employee Headcount
- Average Salary
- Average Leave Balance
- Employees with High Leave Balance
- Job Title Distribution
- Gender Distribution
- Age Demographics
- Qualification vs Salary Analysis
- Cumulative Hiring Trend

---

##  Business Problem

HR departments often manage employee information across multiple files, making it difficult to generate meaningful insights quickly. This dashboard centralizes workforce data into a single interactive report, helping organizations monitor employee metrics, understand workforce trends, and support strategic HR decisions.

---

##  Dashboard Features

###  KPI Cards
- Total Employee Headcount
- Average Salary
- Average Leave Balance
- Employees with Leave Balance Over 20 Days

###  Employee Count by Job Title
Displays the number of employees across different job roles, helping identify departments with the largest workforce.

###  Workforce Gender Distribution
Visualizes the percentage of male and female employees to understand workforce diversity.

###  Employee Age Demographics
Shows the age distribution of employees, helping HR understand workforce maturity and plan future recruitment.

###  Qualification Level vs Salary Range
Analyzes salary distribution based on employee education levels including:
- High School Diploma
- Diploma
- Bachelor's Degree
- Master's Degree

###  Cumulative Hiring Trend
Displays the cumulative number of employees hired over time, highlighting the organization's growth and recruitment patterns.

##  Key Insights

- Total workforce consists of **161 employees**.
- Average employee salary is **54.23K**.
- Average leave balance is **16.42 days**.
- **29 employees** have leave balances exceeding 20 days.
- Packaging Associate and Production Operator have the highest employee count.
- The workforce maintains a relatively balanced gender distribution.
- Most employees belong to the **25–35 years** age group.
- Hiring has steadily increased over the years, indicating organizational growth.
